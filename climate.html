When the Migration Loses Its Calendar

East Africa's most commercially valuable tourism product is becoming structurally unreliable. 90 percent of the Mara-Loita migration footprint is gone since 2020. ICPAC has issued a live flood warning covering the exact circuits that drive peak season revenue. Climate disruption from above, luxury infrastructure from below. The Corridor analyses the sovereign economic risk hiding in plain sight and the three moves East Africa cannot defer.
