Privacy Policy

How The Corridor collects, uses and protects your personal data and subscription information.

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

The Corridor is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

Information We Collect

When you subscribe to The Corridor via Substack, Substack collects your email address and subscription preferences. This is governed by Substack's own privacy policy. The Corridor website itself does not collect personal data beyond standard web analytics.

Analytics

We use anonymised web analytics to understand how readers engage with our content. This includes page views, referral sources and time on page. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to The Corridor, you will receive our weekly Monday publication by email via Substack. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. We do not sell, share or rent subscriber email addresses to third parties under any circumstances.

Cookies

This website uses essential cookies for basic functionality and analytics cookies to understand site usage. Please see our Cookie Policy for full details and to manage your preferences.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at thecorridorafrica@gmail.com.

Data Retention

Subscriber data is held only for as long as you remain a subscriber. Upon unsubscription, your data is deleted in accordance with Substack's data retention policies.

Contact

For any privacy-related queries, contact: thecorridorafrica@gmail.com
