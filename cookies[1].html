Manage Cookies

How The Corridor uses cookies and your options for managing cookie preferences on this site.

Last updated: March 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and allow us to understand how readers engage with our content.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies — These are required for the website to function. They cannot be disabled. They include cookies that remember your preferences during a session.

Analytics Cookies — These help us understand how visitors interact with The Corridor by collecting anonymised data such as pages visited and time spent on site. We use this information to improve our content and user experience.

No Advertising Cookies — The Corridor does not use advertising cookies. We do not serve ads and we do not allow advertisers to track readers on this site.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when cookies are set. Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions specific to your browser.

Third-Party Cookies

If you access The Corridor via embedded content or links to third-party platforms such as Substack, those platforms may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy and cookie policies.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
