01

Consent Version and Record Keeping

Every consent decision made on this page is recorded with a timestamp, consent version number and method of consent in your browser's local storage under the key corridor_cookie_consent. This record is used solely to restore your preferences on return visits and to demonstrate compliance with consent obligations under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and GDPR Article 7.

Consent version 1.0 is current as of March 2026. If the categories of cookies used by The Corridor change materially, the version number will increment and you will be asked to review and re-confirm your preferences. Your prior consent record will be preserved in the audit log.

02

Updates to This Policy

The Corridor may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in the cookies we use or in applicable law. The date of the most recent update and the current policy version are displayed at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated to active subscribers by email before they take effect, and a new consent record will be requested where required by law.

03

Relationship to Our Privacy Policy

This Cookie Policy should be read alongside The Corridor's full Privacy Policy, which sets out in full how personal data is collected, used, stored and protected in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and GDPR. The Privacy Policy also contains details of your data subject rights and how to exercise them, including the right to erasure of your consent record.

04

Contact The Editorial Team

For any questions about how The Corridor uses cookies, to request deletion of your consent record, or to exercise any data subject right in connection with cookie-collected data, contact The Editorial Team directly at thecorridorafrica@gmail.com. All enquiries are responded to within 48 hours. Data subject requests are fulfilled within 30 calendar days at no charge.
