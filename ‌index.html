What We Cover

Six lenses. One continent. Every Monday.

⚔️
Conflict & Displacement
How wars, tensions, and political instability redirect global tourism flows, and which African destinations benefit when the world goes looking for alternatives.
💱
Economics & Currency
Recessions, dollar movements, trade policies and how economic shifts affect tourist spending and which African markets gain or lose when the numbers move.
✈️
Connectivity & Aviation
New routes, airline deals, open skies agreements, the infrastructure of tourism movement and what each development means for Africa's accessibility.
🏛️
Policy & Governance
Visa regimes, tourism strategies, government decisions, analysed for their real-world impact on arrivals, revenue, and Africa's competitive position globally.
🌿
Climate & Environment
How climate events, conservation policies, and environmental shifts are reshaping where people travel, and what Africa must do to stay ahead of the change.
🤝
Diplomacy & Trade
Bilateral agreements, summit outcomes, and diplomatic relationships, decoded for what they mean for African tourism investment, infrastructure, and growth.
